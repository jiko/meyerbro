Meyer Brothers Building Co. was the Design/Build General Contractor of this turnkey project.

The building is a four story structure utilizing a prefabricated load bearing metal stud system combined with a concrete structural floor system utilizing an Epicor deck as the building skeleton.

The roof system consists of a single-ply rubber membrane coupled with a pre-finished architectural sheet metal roof system.

Hotel amenities include a restaurant & lounge, indoor pool & spa, meeting areas and access to a city owned conference center and golf course.
