an epicenter for creativity

The building is a single story structure utilizing load bearing cast-on-site tilt-up concrete wall panels.

The roof system consists of a single-ply rubber membrane coupled with a pre-finished architectural sheet metal roof system.

Conference Center amenities include a full service kitchen and banquet facility capable of accommodating approximately 800 people.

A video conference theatre capable of seating approximately 100 people brings this center ahead of the competition.

The center is connected to a Courtyard By Marriott hotel and closely located to a city-owned golf course.
