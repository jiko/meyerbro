revitalizing the community

Redeveloped at a blighted site in Olathe, KS, Heritage Crossing is re-vitalizing “Old West” Olathe with this new neighborhood center.

Built with cast-on-site tilt-up concrete walls and dressed with a variety of stone this building complex brings a fresh look to retail.
