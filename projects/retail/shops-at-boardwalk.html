delivering the right environment

This open-air shopping center, designed with a southwestern motif, provides a pleasant environment through upscale architectural features and landscaping accents.

A mix of cast-in brick, laid-up brick, modular stone, synthetic stucco and traditional concrete tilt-up wall panels delivers a contemporary, attractive look to the center.

The versatility of tilt-up allowed each tenant to achieve a unique appearance while saving construction dollars.

Project details

Square Footage 136,000 S.F.
Location Kansas City, MO
End Use Open-air lifestyle retail shopping center.
Completion Date August 2003
Architectural Features Cast-in brick, laid-up brick, modular stone, synthetic stucco; free-standing dock screens.
