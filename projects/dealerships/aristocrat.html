This new facility dedicated specifically for the Lnd Rover and Jaquar models consists of a two story showroom and sales office building with a separate service building.

The showroom building is constructed using conventional steel framing with various exterior finishes including ACM panels, EIFS and cultured stone veneer.

The service building is constructed using load-bearing architectural site-cast-concrete panels and a conventional framing system.

The exterior finish is an architecturally painted finish.
