The roar of power and look of excellence

This state-of-the-art dealership facility was a design/build project
utilizing architectural site-cast concrete walls with a conventional
steel framing system.

Exterior finishes combine an EIFS coating directly applied to the concrete walls with cultured stone and tile to create a unique look for the Harley-Davidson customers.

Interior spaces include the showroom, retail clothing & parts sales area, service & parts department, high-performance service area, customer lounge space and administrative offices.
