award winning construction

Meyer Brothers Building Co. was the Design/Build Construction Manager of this turnkey project.

The building is a single story structure utilizing cast on-site concrete tilt-up wall panels with a conventional steel framing system.

Architectural treatments for the tilt-up panels include the use of the Scott Brick System and exposed white Portland Cement concrete.
