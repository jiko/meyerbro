on budget, on time and on target

Meyer Brothers Building Co. was the Design/Build general contractor for this project.

The facility is a single-story structure using a pre-engineered structural steel and standing seam roof system.

The building exterior is covered with an EIFS system to closely match the existing stucco buildings.

The sanctuary seats approximately 1,100 people in a theatre style arrangement to create a connection between the stage and congregation while maintaining close proximity.
