Building a professional look

It takes a professional building company to build a professional building.

This turnkey project is a three-story structure utilizing cast-on-site tilt-up wall panels strengthened with a conventional steel framing system.

The roof is protected by a single-ply rubber membrane with a gravel ballast.

The exterior is decorated with an exposed granite aggregate finish and a reflective glass curtain wall.
