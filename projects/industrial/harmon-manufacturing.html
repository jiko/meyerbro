Our building techniques provide stronger structures that protect your investement and your future.

Every building needs to have a strong structure. In this single-story structure we utilized a conventional structural steel framing system with cast-on-site tilt-up load bearing wall panels.

The office roof is a single story structure using a pre-engineered metal building system provided by Butler Manufacturing.

The exterior wall system is full height metal panels with an interior liner panel.

The roof is a MR-24 standing seam panel with a galvelume finish.
