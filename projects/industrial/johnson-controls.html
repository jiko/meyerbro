Making space work for you

This facility is a single story structure that utilized modern technology to erect a building in a timely fashion.

Cast-on-site concrete tilt-up wall panels and a conventional steel framing system provide strength and flexibility without putting a pinch on the wallet.

The roof is a combinationg of a single-ply rubber membrane and a gravel ballast.

The Design/Build team maximized space including offices, restrooms, a training room, work-out facility, and a cafateria.

The Design/Build project team consisted of the owner, architect, and general contractor.
