Dave meyer

Dave Meyer was a co-founder of Meyer Brothers Building Company in 1977. Prior to founding Meyer Brothers, Dave began his career with the Comptroller of the Currency upon obtaining his degree from Central Missouri State University in 1969.

This tenure was briefly interrupted with a stint in the U.S. Army from late 1969 until his honorable discharge in 1971. He then served as the Cashier at Broadway National Bank from 1974 until 1977.

This broad financial background has served Meyer Brothers well with Dave’s duties in the areas of development, finance, administration and sales.

Dave has been very involved in the Eastern Jackson County area since moving to Blue Springs in 1973. He is a past member and officer of the Blue Springs Jaycees and the Blue Springs Rotary Club.

Meyer Brothers has been very involved in the Blue Springs Chamber of Commerce with Dave having served a term as President and a term as President of its Economic Development Commission. He has served on the Blue Springs R-4 School District’s Citizen’s Advisory Committee and had three children in the school system.

Most recently, Dave served as the 2007 National Chairman of the Associated Builders & Contractors. Dave is a past president of the Association for Industrial Development in the City of Independence, Missouri. He is the past President and continues to serve on the Board of the Blue Springs School of Economics.

roger Meyer

Roger Meyer has been associated with the construction industry since 1973, and since 1977 has served as President of Meyer Brothers Building Company. His areas of concentration are administration, and sales & marketing of commercial, institutional, and industrial construction projects.

Roger received a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business Administration from Central Missouri State University in 1971.

In 2007, Roger served on the Executive Board of the Lee’s Summit Economic Development Council. Roger is a member of the Associated Building Contractors, and the Blue Springs Economic Development Council.

Currently, Roger is the Chairman for the Lee's Summit EDC.

Mark Meyer

Mark Meyer began working in the field on the building crews during holiday and summer breaks from college when Meyer Brothers was founded. That experience soon led him to become responsible for project management, sales, and administration within the organization today.

In 1983 he graduated from Southwest Missouri State University in Springfield, Missouri earning a Bachelor of Science degree in Construction Technology. That following June he joined his two brothers and helped to establish what Meyer Brothers Building Company has become today.

Mark and his wife Laura reside in Blue Springs, Missouri and have two children. Currently Mark is a member of the Independence Chamber of Commerce, and serving as chairman of the new building site development committee at Timothy Lutheran Church in Blue Springs.

Robert Wolf

Graduating with a B.S. from Iowa State University, Robert is no stranger to construction. He has served as the C.O.O. and was a founder of Septagon Industries. He also was President of the Septagon Construction Company located out of Sedalia, Mo.

Robert has a passion for people. He was in Human Resources and Sales for Butler Manufacturing Company, and was the Personnel Administrator for the Cummins Engineering Company.

Robert is a past president of Sedalia United Way, Sedalia Chamber of Commerce; Founder and past president of Sedalia Junior Achievement; College of Business Advisory Board - Iowa State University; Advisory Board – Past Chairman, Central Missouri State University College of Business. Robert has received the Volunteer Economic Development Award from the State of Missouri.

Kasey Graham

Kasey has over 12 years of experience in the construction industry.

He has worked on a variety of construction projects, including the Sprint World Headquarters Campus which was a project of over 3 million square feet of new construction.

He also was part of the Applebee’s International Headquarters, which was an office building project on a 30 acre site with a construction cost of $30,000,000.

As Operations Manager of Meyer Brothers Building Company, Kasey is responsible for the day to day activities of the construction division including project management and field operations.

Kasey received a Bachelor of Science in Finance from the University of Tulsa.

Tim Righi

Tim Righi began his career in Ames, Iowa working on concrete crews before moving into project management and estimating.

In 1992 Tim moved to the Kansas City area to work on a project with the National Weather Service in several positions.

In 2000, Tim joined the Meyer Brothers team, and is Chief Estimator

Tim is a graduate of Iowa State University in Ames, Iowa.

He received a Bachelor of Science degree in Construction Engineering in 1991. The same year he completed the Engineer In Training exam.
